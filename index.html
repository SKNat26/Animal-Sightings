Our Goal

Animals live everywhere; in the city, caves, oceans, skies, and much more. However, what if there was an website that allows users to immediately receieve information about these animals. With one picture (and a name if possible), we will be able to create a network of people sharing animal reports! Our special blog will get these recorded submissions and you will be able to communicate with other users! Grow a community and share your animals!
